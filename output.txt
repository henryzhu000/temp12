Create a custom truststore instead of editing the global one:

keytool -importcert -alias digicert \
    -file DigiCertGlobalRootG2.crt \
    -keystore ./truststore.jks


Then in your Java app:

-Djavax.net.ssl.trustStore=truststore.jks
-Djavax.net.ssl.trustStorePassword=changeit


That way, it only affects your dev app, not the whole syste
